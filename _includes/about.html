Who we are?

Time2Core is a collaboration of two people from worlds apart.

One of them is a PR-mastermind from Moscow, already a player in this business - Gleb Crabz (ex "Goodpeople" promo), famous by his franchise "Viva La Hardcore". The second one is a pragmatic PM from St. Petersburg - Roman Prime, mostly known as highly proficient hardcore DJ PR1ME with over ten years of career.

Together they've gathered their skills to grow up a hardcore scene in St. Petersburg to the next level.
